## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
We may collect:
- **User-provided data** such as your email address (only when you contact us for support)
- **Subscription information** provided through Google Play (purchase status only — we do **not** collect payment details)

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
The app may collect:
- **Device information** (device model, OS version)
- **App usage data** (quiz attempts, study progress, flashcard usage)
- **Crash logs and diagnostics** (via third-party analytics)

We do **not** collect personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it.

### 1.3 Children’s Privacy
PMP Study Hub does **not** target children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.  
If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal data, please contact us so we can remove it immediately.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide app features (quizzes, flashcards, study tracking)
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Fix bugs and prevent crashes
- Manage premium subscriptions
- Respond to user support requests

---

## 3. Data Storage & Retention

- Study progress and activity data are stored **locally on your device**
- Subscription data is managed by **Google Play**, not by us
- Support emails are kept only as long as needed to assist you

We do **not** store personal data on our own servers.

---

## 4. Sharing of Information

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

We may share **anonymous and aggregated** usage data with analytics services for performance improvement.
